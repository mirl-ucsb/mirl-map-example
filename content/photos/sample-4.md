The black stems and bare ground read as damage, but this is upkeep: stretches of the mesa are cleared and burned on purpose to set back non-native growth and give the native scrub a chance to return. Behind it a campus tower stands half-dissolved in the marine fog, the restored coast and the built campus sharing the same few hundred meters.
