Coyote brush and coastal sage have grown up around the warning sign until it is nearly part of the hillside, a yellow rectangle you notice only once you are close enough to ignore it. It is how the institution speaks to an edge it cannot hold: it cannot stop the cliff from failing, so it posts a notice and hands the risk to whoever reads it.
